About

Welcome to ZKRecipes, where the love for food meets the spice of adventure! Created by Zuha Parkar and Khushi Singh—two best friends and students on a mission to spice up your kitchen. Think of us as your foodie travel guides, bringing the magic of Gulf flavors right to your table.

Whether you're looking for traditional national dishes or unique recipes that represent the diversity of Gulf cuisine, ZKRecipes is here to guide you. We believe in preserving culinary traditions while making them accessible to everyone.

We’re here to show you that cooking Gulf dishes isn’t just about following recipes—it’s about embracing the joy of food, experimenting with spices, and sharing meals that bring people together. Whether you’re a pro chef or a total newbie,ZKRecipes is your cheerleader in the kitchen.

Meet the Founders

Zuha Parkar: The spice enthusiast and recipe experimenter who can turn any pantry staple into a masterpiece. When she’s not cooking, she’s reading up on food history or sketching her next recipe idea.

Khushi Singh: The flavor balancer and plating perfectionist. Khushi brings her love for art and culture to every dish she creates. She’s also the resident photographer behind those drool-worthy food shots!

Join the Fun

Ready to embark on this flavor-packed journey? Grab your apron, channel your inner foodie, and let’s cook up some Gulf magic together. Who knows? You might just discover your new favorite dish (or at least have a blast trying)!
