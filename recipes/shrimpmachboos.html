ARABIAN DYNAMITE

Taste of the Gulf

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 40 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 500g shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Oil for frying
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp machboos spice mix
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Chopped cilantro & green onions (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Marinate shrimp in buttermilk for 15 minutes.
  2. Mix flour, cornstarch, salt, pepper, and paprika in a bowl.
  3. Coat shrimp in the flour mixture and deep fry until crispy. Set aside.
  4. In a pan, sauté onions and garlic until golden.
  5. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, machboos spice, turmeric, and cinnamon. Cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add rice and chicken broth, bring to a boil, then simmer covered for 20 minutes.
  7. In a bowl, mix yogurt, mayonnaise, sriracha, and honey to make the dynamite sauce.
  8. Toss fried shrimp in the dynamite sauce.
  9. Serve shrimp over the machboos rice, garnished with cilantro and green onions.
