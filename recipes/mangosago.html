MANGO SAGO

Taste of the Gulf

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup small tapioca pearls (sago)
  • 2 ripe mangoes (peeled and diced)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup cream or coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp saffron threads
  • Ice cubes (for chilling)
  • Mango cubes and mint (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the tapioca pearls in boiling water for 10–15 minutes or until translucent. Drain and rinse under cold water.
  2. Blend the mangoes with milk, cream, sugar, and saffron until smooth.
  3. Mix the blended mango mixture with the cooked sago pearls.
  4. Chill the mixture for at least 30 minutes in the fridge.
  5. Serve cold in bowls or glasses, topped with fresh mango cubes and mint.
