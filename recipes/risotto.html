MADRISOTTO

Taste of the Gulf

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 35 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 tsp madrouba spice mix (turmeric, cumin, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • 1 pinch saffron threads soaked in 2 tbsp warm water
  • 4 cups chicken broth (warmed)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan crisp (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Rub the chicken breasts with madrouba spice mix, salt, and a drizzle of olive oil. Set aside.
  2. In a skillet, pan-fry the chicken on medium heat for 6–7 minutes per side or until fully cooked. Slice into strips or cubes and keep warm.
  3. In a saucepan, heat 1 tbsp olive oil. Sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  4. Add Arborio rice and stir for 2 minutes until slightly toasted.
  5. Pour in the saffron water and begin adding warm chicken broth one ladle at a time, stirring until absorbed before adding more.
  6. Repeat the process for 20–25 minutes until the rice is creamy and al dente.
  7. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Spoon the risotto into bowls and top with the spiced chicken pieces. Garnish with Parmesan crisp if desired.
