BUREK

Taste of the Gulf

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time:30 mins

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 package of phyllo dough (about 12 sheets)
  • 1 lb ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup parsley, chopped
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • Butter or olive oil for brushing

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and sauté the onions until soft and translucent.
  3. Add the ground beef or lamb, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper, and cook until the meat is browned.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the chopped parsley. Let the filling cool slightly.
  5. Lay one sheet of phyllo dough on a clean surface and brush it lightly with butter or olive oil. Place another sheet on top and repeat this process with 4-5 sheets.
  6. Spread a portion of the meat mixture evenly along one edge of the phyllo dough, then roll it up into a tight log or spiral shape.
  7. Place the rolled burek on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Brush the top with egg wash (whisked egg) for a golden finish.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy.
  9. Remove from the oven and allow it to cool slightly before slicing and serving.
