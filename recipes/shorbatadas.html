SHORBAT ADAS

Taste of the Gulf

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time:30 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the diced carrot, celery, and chopped tomato to the pot. Cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Stir in the cumin, coriander, turmeric, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the spices become fragrant.
  4. Add the rinsed lentils and broth to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  5. Once the lentils are cooked, use an immersion blender to puree the soup directly in the pot, or transfer it to a blender in batches to achieve your desired consistency (smooth or slightly chunky).
  6. Add lemon juice and adjust seasoning with more salt and pepper if needed.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley or cilantro and a drizzle of pomegranate molasses if desired.
