HALWA BAHRAINI

Taste of the Gulf

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time:40 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 1/2 tsp saffron strands
  • 1 tsp rose water
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped mixed nuts (pistachios, almonds, cashews)
  • Red food coloring (optional)

Steps

  1. Dissolve cornstarch in water and set aside.
  2. In a pan, mix sugar and 1 cup of water, then bring it to a boil.
  3. Add the cornstarch mixture slowly while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Cook the mixture on medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens and becomes glossy.
  5. Add ghee gradually, followed by saffron, rose water, and cardamom powder.
  6. Mix in food coloring (if using) and chopped nuts.
  7. Pour into a greased dish and let it cool completely before cutting into squares.
