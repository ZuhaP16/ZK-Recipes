MASHKOOL

Taste of the Gulf

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 40 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 5 potatoes
  • 1/2 cup cooked lentils
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 cups water or broth
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (for garnish)
  • 1/4 cup fried onions (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Rinse basmati rice and soak for 20 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and sauté sliced onions and potatoes until caramelized.
  3. Add cumin, coriander, turmeric, black pepper, cinnamon, and salt.
  4. Stir in the cooked lentils and mix well.
  5. Add the soaked rice and pour in water or broth.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cover.
  7. Cook for 20 minutes until the rice is tender and fluffy.
  8. Fluff with a fork and garnish with cilantro and fried onions.
  9. Serve warm with yogurt or a side salad.
