KEBAB SLIDERS

Taste of the Gulf

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Servings: 6 sliders

Ingredients

  • 500g ground lamb or beef
  • 1 small onion (grated)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 6 small slider buns
  • 1/2 cup tahini sauce
  • 1/4 cup pickled onions
  • 1/4 cup arugula
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix ground meat, onion, garlic, salt, pepper, cumin, cinnamon, paprika, and parsley.
  2. Form small kebab patties and refrigerate for 15 minutes.
  3. Heat a grill or pan with olive oil and cook patties for 3-4 minutes per side until cooked through.
  4. Toast slider buns and spread tahini sauce on each bun.
  5. Assemble sliders with arugula, grilled kebab patties, and pickled onions.
  6. Serve warm with extra tahini sauce on the side.
