KABSA

Taste of the Gulf

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 1 hr

Servings: 4-6

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 medium tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 carrot (grated)
  • 1/4 cup raisins (optional)
  • 1/4 cup slivered almonds (toasted, for garnish)
  • 4 cups chicken broth or water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  • 1 dried black lime (loomi), pierced (optional)

Steps

  1. Rinse the basmati rice in cold water and soak for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute, then add the chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the pureed tomatoes, tomato paste, and all the spices. Cook for 5 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Add grated carrot, raisins (if using), and chicken broth or water. Bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 30-40 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked.
  7. Remove the chicken and set aside. Add the soaked and drained rice to the pot.
  8. Place the chicken on top of the rice, cover, and cook on low heat for 20 minutes until the rice is tender.
  9. Fluff the rice with a fork, garnish with toasted almonds, and serve hot.
