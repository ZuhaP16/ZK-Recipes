ZAFRANI CHAI

Taste of the Gulf

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 1 tbsp black tea leaves or 2 tea bags
  • 4-5 saffron strands
  • 2 green cardamom pods, slightly crushed
  • 2 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a saucepan and add saffron and cardamom.
  2. Let it simmer for 2–3 minutes until aromatic.
  3. Add tea leaves and boil for another 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in milk and sugar, then simmer for 3–4 minutes until well infused.
  5. Strain into cups and serve hot, optionally garnished with a saffron strand.
