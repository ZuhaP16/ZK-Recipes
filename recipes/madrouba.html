MADROUBA

Taste of the Gulf

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 1 hr

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (bone-in or boneless)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee or butter
  • 5 cups water or chicken broth
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice and soak for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat ghee and sauté onions until golden. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add chicken, spices, salt, and tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes soften and chicken is browned.
  4. Stir in yogurt and cook for another 5 minutes on medium heat.
  5. Add the soaked rice and pour in water or broth. Bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer.
  6. Stir occasionally as the rice breaks down and becomes creamy. Cook for about 45 minutes to an hour.
  7. Once the consistency is porridge-like and the chicken is tender, remove from heat.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve warm.
