BAKLAVA

Taste of the Gulf

Prep Time: 25 mins

Cook Time: 45 mins

Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • 1 package phyllo pastry (thawed)
  • 2 cups mixed nuts (walnuts, pistachios, or almonds), finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup melted butter
  • For syrup: 1 cup sugar + 1/2 cup water + 1/4 cup honey + 1 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp rose or orange blossom water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Mix chopped nuts with cinnamon in a bowl.
  3. Layer 8 sheets of phyllo dough in the pan, brushing each sheet with butter.
  4. Sprinkle a layer of nut mixture over the phyllo. Add 4 more phyllo sheets (buttered), then more nuts. Repeat until nuts are finished, ending with 8 sheets of phyllo.
  5. Cut the assembled baklava into diamond or square shapes before baking.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes or until golden and crisp.
  7. While it bakes, prepare syrup: boil sugar, water, honey, and lemon juice for 8–10 minutes. Stir in rose/orange blossom water and cool.
  8. Pour cooled syrup over hot baklava immediately after removing from oven. Let it soak and cool before serving.
