SHUWA

Taste of the Gulf

Prep Time: 1 day

Cook Time: 6–8 hrs

Servings: 6–8

Ingredients

  • 2 kg lamb or goat meat (with bone)
  • 4 tbsp Omani spice mix (baharat)
  • 6 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Banana leaves or foil for wrapping

Steps

  1. Mix baharat, garlic, ginger, vinegar, lemon juice, and salt to form a marinade.
  2. Rub the marinade thoroughly over the lamb and refrigerate for 24 hours.
  3. Wrap the marinated meat tightly in banana leaves or foil.
  4. Slow cook in a preheated oven at 150°C (300°F) for 6–8 hours until the meat is fall-apart tender.
  5. Unwrap and serve with spiced rice or flatbread.
