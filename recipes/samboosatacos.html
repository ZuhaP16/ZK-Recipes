SAMBOOSA TACOS

Taste of the Gulf

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 small tortillas
  • 1 cup ground beef or chicken
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a pan, heat oil and sauté onions until golden. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  2. Add ground meat, cumin, coriander, chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Cook until fully done.
  3. Stir in fresh cilantro and set the filling aside.
  4. Heat tortillas in a dry pan until slightly crisp.
  5. Fill each tortilla with the cooked meat and shredded cheese, then fold into a taco shape.
  6. Mix yogurt with lime juice and drizzle over the tacos before serving.
