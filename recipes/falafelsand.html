FALAFEL SANDWICH

Taste of the Gulf

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups dried chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 cup fresh parsley
  • 1 cup fresh cilantro
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • 4 pita breads
  • Tahini sauce, lettuce, tomato, and pickles for serving

Steps

  1. Drain chickpeas and blend with onion, garlic, herbs, spices, and baking soda until coarse and sticky.
  2. Shape mixture into small balls or patties and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry falafel until golden brown and crisp.
  4. Warm pita bread and fill with falafel, lettuce, tomato, pickles, and a drizzle of tahini sauce.
  5. Serve immediately while warm and crisp.
